Past Costumes

Here are some of my earlier projects! I hope to expand this collection and my talents as the year goes on!

Plague Doctor

This was my first foray into more complex halloween costumes. There were several iterations of this design, all of them making use of a small handheld smoke machine. Originally, I wanted the smoke to descend from the mask, but that resulted in breathing and visibility issues, so I changed it to come from a clawed glove a la Freddy Kruger. Then, I sourced a lovely wooden staff from a Renfaire, afixed a 3d printed Crow's head to the top of it, and put holes in the eyes, so that it would "cry" smoke.

Scarecrow

This was another interesting project, and my first attempt at incorporating LEDs into my costume. I purchased a couple yards of burlap from michaels, and a cheap straw hat as well as some string to give the "strawman" effect. The LED's are wired to a simple coincell battery pack, and are poked through the fibers of the burlap, which I can see through like a screen. Additionally, I brought back the smoke machine and incorporated it into my lamp, which has a PVC tube up my sleeve connected to the lamp, so that it blows smoke and creates a spooky effect. The rake is just a kids rake from Ace hardware.
